# houserules.md — Pelangi Hostel House Rules

> **Official hostel policies and rules**

## Core House Rules

### 1. Quiet Hours
**Time:** 11:00 PM - 7:00 AM
**Why:** Respect for guests who are sleeping
**Details:**
- Keep noise to minimum in dormitory areas
- Use headphones for music/videos
- Move conversations to common areas
- No loud activities (parties, gatherings)

**Enforcement:**
- First violation: Friendly reminder
- Repeated violations: Staff intervention
- Serious disruption: Early checkout (no refund)

### 2. No Smoking
**Where:** All indoor areas (100% smoke-free hostel)
**Alternative:** Designated smoking area outside
**Details:**
- No cigarettes, vaping, e-cigarettes indoors
- Use outdoor designated area only
- Dispose of cigarette butts properly

**Penalty:** RM50 cleaning fee if smoking detected indoors

### 3. No Outside Guests
**Rule:** Only registered guests allowed in dormitory areas
**Why:** Security and privacy of paying guests
**Details:**
- Visitors can wait in lobby/reception
- No overnight guests unless they book a capsule
- Staff ID check for unknown persons

**Exception:** With prior approval from staff

### 4. Cleanliness & Hygiene
**Shared Spaces:**
- Clean up after yourself (kitchen, bathrooms, common areas)
- Return dishes to kitchen (clean)
- Flush toilets, wipe sinks
- Dispose of garbage properly

**Personal Space:**
- Keep your capsule tidy
- Use designated storage areas
- Don't leave belongings in shared spaces

**Showers:**
- Wipe down shower area after use
- Use provided mats
- Keep toiletries in your capsule (no storage in bathroom)

### 5. Security & Safety
**Key Cards:**
- Don't share your access card
- Report lost cards immediately (RM20 replacement fee)
- Don't prop doors open

**Valuables:**
- Use provided lockers (bring your own padlock)
- Hostel not responsible for lost/stolen items
- Report suspicious activity to staff

**Emergency:**
- Know emergency exit locations
- Don't block fire exits
- Follow staff instructions in emergencies

### 6. Kitchen Use
**Rules:**
- Clean all dishes, utensils, and surfaces after use
- Don't leave food out overnight
- Label your food (use stickers provided)
- Respect "first in, first out" in fridge

**Not Allowed:**
- Cooking strong-smelling foods (e.g., durian, fish)
- Using hostel appliances for commercial purposes
- Storing alcohol in shared fridge (keep in your capsule)

**Penalty:** RM20 cleaning fee for not cleaning up

### 7. Alcohol Policy
**Allowed:**
- Moderate drinking in common areas (not dormitories)
- Bring your own (no sales on premises)
- Drink responsibly

**Not Allowed:**
- Drinking in capsule areas
- Excessive drinking causing disturbance
- Underage drinking (min 21 years, ID check)

**Enforcement:**
- Disruptive behavior → Immediate removal (no refund)

### 8. Check-In/Check-Out Times
**Check-In:** 2:00 PM onwards
**Check-Out:** 12:00 PM (noon)

**Early Check-In:**
- Subject to availability (ask at reception)
- May require half-day rate

**Late Check-Out:**
- Subject to availability
- RM20 per hour after 12:00 PM
- Must arrange with staff before checkout time

### 9. Guest Conduct
**Expected Behavior:**
- Respect other guests and staff
- Follow cultural sensitivity (diverse guests)
- No harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior
- Respect privacy (no taking photos of other guests without permission)

**Zero Tolerance:**
- Theft
- Violence or threats
- Drug use
- Harassment or hate speech

**Consequence:** Immediate eviction, police involvement if necessary

### 10. Damage & Loss
**Responsibility:**
- Guests liable for damage to property
- Intentional damage → Full replacement cost + RM100 service fee
- Accidental damage → Report immediately, reasonable charges apply

**Deposit:**
- RM50 deposit at check-in (refunded at checkout if no damage)
- Deductions made for damages, missing items, or unpaid fees

## Additional Guidelines

### WiFi Use
- Free WiFi provided
- Fair use policy (no torrenting, no illegal downloads)
- Network name and password at reception
- Report connection issues to staff

### Laundry
- Self-service laundry available (RM5/load)
- Washer: Free to use (bring your own detergent)
- Dryer: RM5 per cycle
- Don't leave clothes in machines (will be removed after 30 min)

### Noise Levels (Daytime)
- Reasonable conversation levels OK
- Use headphones for media
- Take phone calls in common areas (not capsules)
- No loud music at any time in sleeping areas

### Shoes Policy
- Remove shoes at dormitory entrance (shoe racks provided)
- Slippers/socks OK inside
- Keeps floors clean and reduces noise

### Guest Modifications
**Not Allowed:**
- Moving furniture
- Altering capsule bed setup
- Installing personal items (hooks, shelves, etc.)
- Tampering with electrical systems

### Lost & Found
- Check with reception for lost items
- Found items held for 30 days
- Unclaimed items donated to charity

## Rule Updates

**Notification:** Guests notified of rule changes via:
- Email (for future bookings)
- Notice at reception
- App notifications (if using Rainbow check-in)

**Effective Date:** Rule changes effective 7 days after notification

## Questions About Rules

**Common Questions:**

**Q: Can I bring food to my capsule?**
A: Yes, but no strong-smelling food. Use kitchen for meals.

**Q: Can I have guests visit me in the common area?**
A: Yes, but they cannot enter dormitory areas without booking.

**Q: What if I need to leave luggage after checkout?**
A: Luggage storage available for RM5/day (subject to availability).

**Q: Can I request a specific capsule?**
A: We'll try to accommodate, but not guaranteed. Assigned at check-in.

**Q: What if my roommate is noisy?**
A: Contact staff immediately. We'll address the issue.
