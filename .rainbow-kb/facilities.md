> **What Pelangi Hostel offers to guests**

## Sleeping Facilities

### Capsule Pods

**Standard Capsule:**
- Single bed (100cm × 200cm)
- Privacy curtain/blind
- Reading light (LED, adjustable)
- Power outlet (2-pin, universal adapter available)
- USB charging port
- Personal air conditioning vent
- Small shelf for personal items
- Clothes hooks

**Premium Capsule:**
- Larger space (120cm × 220cm)
- Window view
- All standard capsule features
- Extra storage shelf

**Bedding Provided:**
- Fresh linens (changed for every guest)
- Pillow
- Light blanket (extra available on request)

**Not Provided:**
- Towels (bring your own or rent RM5)
- Earplugs (available for purchase RM2)
