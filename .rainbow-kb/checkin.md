# checkin.md — Check-In Process Details

> **Step-by-step check-in procedures for guests**

## Check-In Overview

**Check-In Time:** 2:00 PM onwards
**Location:** Front desk / Digital self-check-in kiosk
**Duration:** ~5-10 minutes
**Requirements:** Valid ID, booking confirmation, payment

## Pre-Arrival (Optional)

### 1. Booking Confirmation
**What You'll Receive:**
- Booking confirmation email
- Booking reference number
- Check-in instructions
- Digital check-in link (via Rainbow)

**What to Bring:**
- Valid government-issued ID (passport, IC, driver's license)
- Booking confirmation (digital or printed)
- Payment method

### 2. Early Communication
**Contact Options:**
- WhatsApp: +60 12-345-6789 (example)
- Email: info@pelangihostel.com
- Rainbow chatbot (24/7)

**You Can Ask About:**
- Early check-in availability
- Special requests (lower/upper bunk, quiet area, etc.)
- Directions to hostel
- Nearby attractions

## Check-In Methods

### Method 1: Digital Check-In (Recommended)

**Step 1: Receive Check-In Link**
- Sent via email/WhatsApp 24 hours before arrival
- Click link to start Rainbow-guided check-in

**Step 2: Fill Information**
- Personal details verification
- ID photo upload (optional, saves time at desk)
- Payment information
- Emergency contact

**Step 3: Review & Confirm**
- House rules acknowledgment
- Payment summary
- Special requests

**Step 4: Receive Access Details**
- Capsule assignment
- Access code for key card collection
- WiFi details
- Important information

**Step 5: Arrive & Collect Key**
- Go to front desk
- Show digital confirmation
- Collect key card
- Receive welcome brief (5 min)

### Method 2: Front Desk Check-In

**Step 1: Arrival**
- Arrive at front desk
- Present ID and booking confirmation

**Step 2: Registration**
- Fill out registration form
- Verify booking details
- Provide emergency contact

**Step 3: Payment**
- Pay for stay + RM50 deposit
- Receive receipt

**Step 4: Orientation**
- Receive key card
- Staff shows you to capsule
- Quick facility tour
- WiFi password provided

**Step 5: Settle In**
- Locate your capsule
- Store belongings in locker
- Explore facilities

## What Happens at Check-In

### Information Collected

**Required:**
- Full name (as per ID)
- Nationality
- ID number (passport/IC)
- Contact number
- Emergency contact

**Optional:**
- Email (for receipts/updates)
- Special requests
- Dietary restrictions (if cooking in kitchen)

**Privacy:** All information encrypted and secured. See privacy policy.

### Documents Needed

**Local Guests (Malaysian):**
- IC (Identity Card) OR
- Driver's License OR
- Passport

**International Guests:**
- Valid passport
- Visa (if applicable)

**Copies:** We may photocopy ID for records (legal requirement)

### Payment at Check-In

**What to Pay:**
1. **Full stay cost** OR first night (for long stays)
2. **Security deposit** (RM50, refundable)

**Total Example (3 nights, weekday):**
- 3 nights × RM45 = RM135
- Deposit = RM50
- **Total: RM185**
- (Deposit refunded at checkout if no issues)

### Capsule Assignment

**How It Works:**
- Assigned based on availability and preferences
- We try to accommodate requests (not guaranteed)
- Gender-specific or mixed dorms (specify preference)

**Preferences We Consider:**
- Lower vs upper bunk
- Near bathroom vs away
- Quiet area vs social area
- Away from entrance/exit

**Can't Guarantee Specific Capsule:**
- But we note your preferences
- If your preference available, you get it!

**What It Opens:**

**Important:**
- Don't share your key card

**24/7 Access:**
- Use key card to enter building
- Late arrivals: Use night bell (staff notified)

**Security:**
- CCTV in common areas (not in capsules/bathrooms)
- Staff on duty 24/7
- Emergency contact: [Number]

## First-Time Guests

### What to Expect (Capsule Hotel Newbies)

**Your Capsule:**
- Private sleeping pod (bed)
- Curtain/blind for privacy
- Reading light, power outlet
- Air conditioning vent
- Storage shelf inside

**Not in Capsule:**
- Bathroom (shared, clean, modern)
- Kitchen (shared)
- Lockers (bring padlock or rent RM5)

**Size:**
- Enough space to sit up
- Longer than typical bed
- Cozy but not cramped

### Common Concerns Addressed

**"Is it safe?"**
- Yes! 24/7 staff, CCTV, key card access
- Lockers for valuables
- Other guests are travelers like you

**"Is it clean?"**
- Professional cleaning daily
- Fresh linens for every guest
- Regular deep cleaning

**"Will it be noisy?"**
- Quiet hours enforced (11 PM - 7 AM)
- Sound insulation in capsules
- Staff address noise issues immediately

**"What about privacy?"**
- Curtain/blind for your capsule
- No one can see in when closed
- Gender-specific options available

## Special Situations

### Early Check-In

**Before 2 PM:**
- Subject to availability (not guaranteed)
- May require half-day rate (RM25)
- Call ahead to check availability
- Free if capsule already clean and available

### Late Check-In

**After 10 PM:**
- Please notify us in advance
- Night staff will assist
- Self-check-in option available (digital)
- Access code provided via WhatsApp

**After Midnight:**
- Call/WhatsApp upon arrival
- Night bell at entrance
- Staff will come to assist

### Group Check-In

**4+ People:**
- One person can check in for group
- Need all IDs and payment
- Capsules assigned together (if possible)
- Group briefing in common area

### Accessible Needs

**Mobility:**
- Ground floor capsules available (request in advance)
- No steps to ground floor units
- Accessible bathroom available

**Other Needs:**
- Contact us before arrival
- We'll do our best to accommodate

## After Check-In

### Immediate Steps

1. **Go to Your Capsule**
   - Note capsule number
   - Test key card
   - Locate light switches and outlets

2. **Secure Valuables**
   - Use locker (bring padlock)
   - Don't leave valuables in capsule
   - Passport, electronics, money → locker

3. **Connect to WiFi**
   - Network: Pelangi_Guest
   - Password: [provided at check-in]
   - Free unlimited (fair use policy)

4. **Explore Facilities**
   - Locate bathrooms
   - Find kitchen
   - Common areas
   - Emergency exits

5. **Read House Rules**
   - Provided in welcome packet
   - Also in this KB (houserules.md)
   - Ask staff if questions

### Your First Night

**Settling In:**
- Store luggage under bed or in locker
- Hang clothes on hooks inside capsule
- Shoes at dormitory entrance (shoe rack)
- Meet other guests in common area

**Need Help?**
- Front desk staff available 24/7
- Contact via reception desk
- WhatsApp for non-urgent questions
- Rainbow chatbot for quick info

## FAQs

**Q: Can I check in before 2 PM?**
A: Subject to availability. Call ahead to check. May require half-day rate.

**Q: What if I arrive late at night?**
A: No problem! Notify us in advance. Night staff available or self-check-in option.

**Q: Do I need to print my booking confirmation?**
A: No, digital version on phone is fine.

**Q: Can someone else check in for me?**
A: Yes, but they need your ID photo/copy and booking confirmation.

**Q: What if I don't have a padlock for the locker?**
A: We rent them for RM5 (one-time, keep it for your stay).

**Q: Can I choose my capsule?**
A: We assign based on availability and preferences, but can't guarantee specific capsule number.

**Q: Is there luggage storage before check-in time?**
A: Yes, RM5/day if you arrive early and capsule not ready.

**Q: Do I get a tour of the hostel?**
A: Yes, brief orientation included in check-in process.
