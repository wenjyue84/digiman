> **Quick answers to common guest questions**

## Before Booking

### About Capsule Hotels

**Q: What is a capsule hotel?**
A: A capsule hotel provides private sleeping pods (capsules) instead of traditional rooms. Each capsule is like a cozy bed-sized space with privacy curtain, reading light, and personal outlets. Bathrooms and common areas are shared.

**Q: Is it safe and private?**
A: Yes! Each capsule has a privacy curtain, 24/7 staff, CCTV in common areas, and key card access. You can lock your valuables in personal lockers.

**Q: How big is a capsule?**
A: Standard capsules are 100cm wide Ã— 200cm long (about the size of a single bed). Premium capsules are slightly larger (120cm Ã— 220cm). You can sit up comfortably inside.

**Q: Can two people share a capsule?**
A: No, capsules are designed for one person only. Each person needs their own capsule.

**Q: Do capsules have doors or curtains?**
A: Privacy curtain/blind that you can close for complete privacy.

### Booking & Pricing

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: RM45-55/night for standard capsules (weekday/weekend). RM60-70 for premium. See payment.md for full pricing.

**Q: Is there a deposit?**
A: Yes, RM50 security deposit (refundable at checkout if no damages/fees).

**Q: What's included in the price?**
A: Bed linens, WiFi, air conditioning, personal locker, kitchen use, hot showers, basic toiletries (soap/shampoo).

**Q: What's NOT included?**
A: Towels (bring your own or rent RM5), meals (kitchen available), padlock for locker (bring or rent RM5).

**Q: Can I get a discount for long stays?**
A: Yes! 7+ nights get 10% off. 30+ nights get monthly rate (RM900-1200/month). See payment.md.

**Q: Do you accept credit cards?**
A: Yes (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) with 3% processing fee. Also accept cash, e-wallets (free), and bank transfer.

**Q: What's your cancellation policy?**
A: 7+ days notice = full refund. 3-7 days = 50% refund. Less than 3 days = no refund. See payment.md.

## Check-In & Check-Out

### Check-In

**Q: What time is check-in?**
A: 2:00 PM onwards.

**Q: Can I check in early?**
A: Subject to availability (not guaranteed). May require half-day rate (RM25). Call ahead to check.

**Q: What if I arrive late at night?**
A: No problem! Notify us in advance. Night staff available or use digital self-check-in.

**Q: What do I need to bring for check-in?**
A: Valid ID (passport/IC), booking confirmation, payment method.

**Q: Do I need to print my booking confirmation?**
A: No, digital version on your phone is fine.

**Q: Can someone else check in for me?**
A: Yes, but they need your ID (photo/copy) and booking confirmation.

### Check-Out

**Q: What time is check-out?**
A: 12:00 PM (noon).

**Q: Can I check out late?**
A: Subject to availability. RM20/hour after noon. Arrange with staff before checkout time.

**Q: How do I get my deposit back?**
A: At checkout, after capsule inspection. Refunded in full if no issues (damages, missing items, unpaid fees).

**Q: Can I store luggage after checkout?**
A: Yes, RM5/day (subject to space availability, max 3 days).

## Facilities & Amenities

### Internet & Tech

**Q: Is WiFi free?**
A: Yes, free high-speed WiFi (100 Mbps shared) throughout the hostel.

**Q: What's the WiFi password?**
A: Provided at check-in.

**Q: Do capsules have power outlets?**
A: Yes, 2-pin power outlet + USB charging port in each capsule.

**Q: Do I need a power adapter?**
A: Malaysia uses 240V, 3-pin UK-style plugs. Universal adapters available at front desk (free, RM20 deposit).

### Kitchen & Food

**Q: Is breakfast included?**
A: No, but we have a full kitchen you can use (free, 24/7). Cook your own meals or eat at nearby restaurants.

**Q: Can I cook my own food?**
A: Yes! Full kitchen with fridge, microwave, stove, rice cooker, toaster, and all cookware provided.

**Q: Are there restaurants nearby?**
A: Yes, many options within 5-10 minutes walking distance. Also 7-Eleven next door (24/7).

**Q: Can I order food delivery to the hostel?**
A: Yes, Grab Food, Food Panda, etc. all deliver here.

### Bathroom & Laundry

**Q: Are bathrooms shared?**
A: Yes, modern shared bathrooms (separate male/female). Hot water showers 24/7.

**Q: Are towels provided?**
A: No, bring your own or rent for RM5 (one-time, return at checkout).

**Q: Is there laundry?**
A: Yes, self-service. Washer is free (bring detergent). Dryer is RM5/cycle. Also outdoor drying rack (free).

**Q: Where can I buy detergent?**
A: 7-Eleven next door or supermarket 10 min walk. May have small packets in vending machine (RM2).

### Storage & Security

**Q: Where can I store my valuables?**
A: Personal lockers available (bring your own padlock or rent for RM5).

**Q: Is my stuff safe?**
A: We have 24/7 staff, CCTV in common areas, and key card access. Use lockers for valuables. Hostel not responsible for items left unsecured.

**Q: Can I leave my luggage under my bed?**
A: Yes, limited space under bed. Use lockers for valuables.

## House Rules & Policies

### Quiet Hours

**Q: What are quiet hours?**
A: 11:00 PM - 7:00 AM. Keep noise to minimum in sleeping areas during this time.

**Q: Can I watch videos/listen to music in my capsule?**
A: Yes, but use headphones (especially during quiet hours).

### Smoking & Alcohol

**Q: Can I smoke?**
A: Not indoors (100% smoke-free hostel). Designated smoking area outside. RM50 fine if you smoke indoors.

**Q: Can I drink alcohol?**
A: Yes, moderate drinking in common areas (not in capsule areas). Drink responsibly. No disruptive behavior.

### Guests & Visitors

**Q: Can I bring a guest/visitor?**
A: Visitors can wait in lobby/reception. Not allowed in dormitory areas unless they book a capsule.

**Q: Can my friend visit me?**
A: They can meet you in common areas, but not enter sleeping areas without booking.
