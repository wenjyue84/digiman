# payment.md — Payment Policies & Procedures

> **Pricing, payment methods, refunds, and financial policies**

## Pricing Structure

### Capsule Rates (Per Night)

**Standard Capsule:**
- Weekday (Sun-Thu): RM45/night
- Weekend (Fri-Sat): RM55/night
- Weekly rate (7+ nights): RM40/night (weekday avg)
- Monthly rate: RM900/month

**Premium Capsule (Larger, Window View):**
- Weekday: RM60/night
- Weekend: RM70/night
- Weekly rate: RM55/night
- Monthly rate: RM1,200/month

**Prices Include:**
- WiFi
- Air conditioning
- Bed linens and pillow
- Personal reading light
- Power outlet
- Basic toiletries (soap, shampoo)
- Locker use

### Deposits & Fees

**Security Deposit:**
- RM50 (refundable at checkout)
- Covers potential damages, missing items, unpaid fees
- Returned in full if no issues

**Additional Fees:**
- Key card replacement: RM20
- Late checkout (per hour): RM20
- Smoking indoors: RM50 cleaning fee
- Kitchen not cleaned: RM20
- Laundry dryer: RM5/cycle

## Payment Methods

### Accepted Payments

✅ **Cash**
- Malaysian Ringgit (RM)
- Payment at reception

✅ **Credit/Debit Cards**
- Visa, Mastercard, Amex
- 3% processing fee applies

✅ **Online Banking/E-Wallet**
- FPX (Free)
- Touch 'n Go eWallet (Free)
- GrabPay (Free)
- Boost (Free)

✅ **Bank Transfer**
- Direct bank transfer (provide proof)
- Payment must clear before check-in

### Payment Timing

**At Check-In:**
- Full payment for entire stay OR
- First night + deposit (for long stays)

**During Stay (Extended Stay):**
- Weekly payment for extended bookings
- Payment due every 7 days

**At Check-Out:**
- Settlement of any additional charges
- Deposit return (if no deductions)

## Booking & Cancellation

### Booking Deposit (Online Reservations)

**Standard Booking:**
- RM50 deposit to secure booking
- Applied toward total stay cost
- Required within 24 hours of reservation

**Walk-Ins:**
- No advance deposit
- Full payment at check-in

### Cancellation Policy

**More than 7 days before check-in:**
- Full refund minus RM10 processing fee

**3-7 days before check-in:**
- 50% refund

**Less than 3 days before check-in:**
- No refund (deposit forfeited)

**No-show:**
- No refund

**Exception:** Medical emergencies (with doctor's note)

### Modification Policy

**Change dates:**
- Free if more than 7 days before check-in
- RM10 fee if less than 7 days
- Subject to availability

**Change capsule type:**
- Free if upgrading (pay difference)
- Downgrade refund only if 7+ days notice

## Refunds & Returns

### Security Deposit Refund

**Full refund (RM50) if:**
- No damage to property
- All house rules followed
- Capsule clean and tidy
- Key card returned
- No outstanding fees

**Partial refund if:**
- Minor cleaning needed (deduct RM20)
- Key card lost (deduct RM20)
- Small damages (deduct actual cost)

**No refund if:**
- Major damages
- Rule violations requiring cleanup
- Outstanding fees exceed deposit

**Refund Method:**
- Cash (immediate)
- Bank transfer (2-3 business days)
- Original payment method (3-5 business days)

### Booking Refund (Cancellations)

**Processing Time:**
- Cash bookings: Immediate
- Card payments: 7-14 business days
- Bank transfers: 3-5 business days

**Refund Method:**
- Same method as original payment
- Minus processing fees (if applicable)

## Payment Issues & Support

### Payment Declined

**If card declined:**
1. Check with your bank (daily limits, international transactions)
2. Try alternative payment method
3. Use online banking/e-wallet (no processing fee)

**If bank transfer not received:**
1. Provide transaction reference number
2. Allow 1-2 hours for verification
3. Contact staff if urgent

### Disputes

**If you disagree with charges:**
1. Speak with staff immediately
2. Request itemized breakdown
3. Escalate to manager if unresolved
4. Formal complaint: info@pelangihostel.com

**Dispute Resolution:**
- Manager review within 24 hours
- Evidence review (photos, timestamps)
- Fair resolution priority

## FAQs

**Q: Can I pay half now, half later?**
A: For stays 7+ nights, yes (first night + deposit, then weekly payments).

**Q: Do you accept foreign credit cards?**
A: Yes, but your bank may charge foreign transaction fees.

**Q: What if I extend my stay?**
A: Pay for additional nights at check-out or when extending.

**Q: Can I get an invoice for my company?**
A: Yes, provide company details at check-in. Invoice within 24 hours.

**Q: Is breakfast included?**
A: No, but kitchen facilities available (bring your own food or eat nearby).

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No. All fees listed in this document. You pay only what's stated + any optional services.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I leave early?**
A: No, unless medical emergency with doctor's note.

**Q: How do I get my deposit back?**
A: At check-out, after capsule inspection. Cash or transfer.
