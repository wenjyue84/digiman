# Self Check-in Procedure

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Prepare your identification document**
   - **International guests:** Please have your **passport** ready for identity verification
   - **Malaysian guests:** Please have your **IC (identification card)** ready
   - A photo of your passport or IC will be required to complete check-in

2. **Arrive at the entrance**
   - Use the map link: https://maps.app.goo.gl/maLXetUqYS5MtSLD9?g_st=iwb

3. **Enter the door password**
   - Password: **1270#**
   - Enter at door keypad, then push/pull to enter

4. **Go to your assigned capsule area**
   - Refer to your booking confirmation or check-in instructions
   - Capsule numbers are clearly labeled

5. **Watch the capsule usage video** (recommended for first-timers)
   - Video tutorial: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6Ux11oBZaQQ
   - Shows how to use capsule features, curtain, light, outlets

6. **Connect to WiFi**
   - See `checkin-wifi.md` for credentials

7. **If anything doesn't work**
   - Door won't open? Capsule issue? WiFi problem?
   - Contact us immediately via WhatsApp: +60 12-708 8789 (Jay)
