# Lost & Found

## If You Left Something Behind

If you forgot an item after checking out, we can help!

**Contact us immediately:**
- WhatsApp: +60127088789
- Call: +60127088789

**Pickup Options:**
1. **Pickup in person** - Come back during office hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
2. **Shipping** - We can ship to your address (shipping fee applies)
3. **Hold for next visit** - We'll keep it safe for up to 30 days

**Urgent Items (Passport, ID):**
For important documents, we prioritize **immediate** notification and **secure** storage. Contact us ASAP.

**Found Property:**
If someone finds your item in the hostel, we'll contact you using your booking details.

## Lost & Found Process

1. Describe the item (what, color, brand)
2. Tell us where you think you left it (capsule number, common area)
3. We'll search and confirm within 24 hours
4. Arrange pickup or shipping

Items unclaimed after 60 days are donated to charity.
